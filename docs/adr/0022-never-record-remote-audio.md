# Never record remote audio

Remote Audio Sessions will provide only live, end-to-end encrypted Child-to-Guardian streaming and will expose no recording capability. Child Mode publishes one audio track; Guardian Mode is receive-only, never opens its microphone, and offers no talkback, duplex mode, transcript, or save action. Neither endpoint nor backend persists audio, and any future relay may handle only encrypted packets, reducing misuse, legal exposure, and breach impact for ambient audio involving Children and bystanders.
