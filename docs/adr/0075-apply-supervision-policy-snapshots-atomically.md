# Apply Supervision Policy snapshots atomically

Child Mode will acknowledge and persist a Supervision Policy as its last accepted policy only after validating the complete snapshot and successfully activating every required feature section. For schema v3, activation initializes and self-checks each enabled detector before acknowledgement; a disabled detector does not require its model session, while failure of any enabled detector rejects the complete candidate rather than partially applying the policy. Child Mode continues enforcing the previous accepted snapshot, does not advance its applied version, and retries the desired version; a policy version is never reported as partially applied. Candidate download state, if persisted for retry, is distinct from the last accepted policy so that process death or activation failure cannot replace known enforceable offline state.
