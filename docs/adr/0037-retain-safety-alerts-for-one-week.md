# Retain Safety Alerts for one week

Cereveil will retain individual metadata-only Safety Alerts for one week and then delete them without creating an aggregate summary. Guardians may read or archive the associated Guardian Notice state but cannot manually delete, export, search, share, or retain an alert beyond its automatic expiry. Pending Safety Alerts on an offline Child Device also expire after at most one week. This gives Guardians a short incident view without accumulating a long-term safety or behavioral history about a Child.
