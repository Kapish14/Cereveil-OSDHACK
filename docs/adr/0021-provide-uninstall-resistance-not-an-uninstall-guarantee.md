# Provide uninstall resistance, not an uninstall guarantee

As a consumer Play app, Cereveil will intercept ordinary removal and permission-revocation flows through its disclosed parental-control Accessibility use and require Guardian Approval, while monitoring Supervision Health and emitting Tamper Alerts when possible. It will not use Device Admin abuse or claim guaranteed uninstall prevention because Accessibility can be revoked and safe mode, ADB, factory reset, power loss, or connectivity loss cannot be fully controlled or reliably distinguished.
